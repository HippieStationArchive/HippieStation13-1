2 February 2015

Crystalwarrior160 updated:

  • Added cig vendor to speakeasy
  • Added a special cig vendor to brig - contains more robust packets than anywhere else!
  • Cigarettes were tweaked: nicotine addiction nerfed, cigpack contents were changed completely (no cigpacks contain toxic materials now, other than carp classic. Robust cigs actually contain robust chemicals now, too.)
  • Cigarettes can now be grinded in All-in-One Grinder
  • Lit cigs and nicotine will now prevent coughing and nervousness disabilities side effects. Obviously won't remove the disabilities itself.
  • Shady Jim's cigpack can now be acquired through a hacked vendor
  • Fixed security helmet not showing up on mob
  • Computer sprites replaced by newer ones done by Mono! They have directions and stuff. Tell Crystalwarrior160 if any of the computer sprites don't show up or they're acting weird.
